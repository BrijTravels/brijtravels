Tour Overview

Embark on a spiritual journey through the sacred land of Braj, where Lord Krishna spent his divine childhood. This carefully curated 3-day tour takes you through the most significant temples and holy sites in Mathura, Vrindavan, and Gokul.

Day 1: Mathura Darshan

  • Morning visit to Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple
  • Dwarkadheesh Temple darshan
  • Evening Yamuna Aarti at Vishram Ghat

Day 2: Vrindavan Experience

  • Early morning visit to Banke Bihari Temple
  • ISKCON Temple and Prem Mandir visit
  • Evening cultural program and bhajans

Day 3: Gokul & Departure

  • Morning visit to Gokul
  • Nand Baba Temple darshan
  • Afternoon departure
