Tour Overview

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Rajasthan with our comprehensive 6-day tour. Experience the grandeur of palaces, the might of forts, and the vibrant traditions of the royal state.

Day 1-2: Jaipur - The Pink City

  • Amber Fort visit with elephant ride
  • City Palace and Hawa Mahal tour
  • Traditional dinner with cultural show

Day 3-4: Udaipur - City of Lakes

  • City Palace complex exploration
  • Lake Pichola boat ride
  • Vintage car museum visit

Day 5: Jodhpur - The Blue City

  • Mehrangarh Fort tour
  • Jaswant Thada visit
  • Local market exploration

Day 6: Departure

  • Morning heritage walk
  • Souvenir shopping
  • Airport transfer
