ISKCON Temple Vrindavan

Experience the spiritual essence of Krishna consciousness in this magnificent temple

About ISKCON Temple

The ISKCON Temple in Vrindavan, also known as Krishna Balaram Mandir, is one of the most important temples of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness. Founded by Srila Prabhupada, it's a spiritual haven where devotees from around the world gather to worship Lord Krishna.

Temple Significance

The temple is renowned for its beautiful deities of Krishna-Balaram, Radha-Shyamasundar, and Gaura-Nitai. The architecture combines modern and traditional elements, creating a serene atmosphere for spiritual practices.

  • Darshan Hours: 4:30 AM - 12:30 PM & 4:00 PM - 8:30 PM
  • Photography allowed in temple premises
