Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple

Experience the divine birthplace of Lord Krishna, one of the most sacred sites in Mathura

About the Temple

Krishna Janmabhoomi, literally meaning "Krishna's birthplace", is a temple complex in Mathura, Uttar Pradesh. The temple marks the location where Lord Krishna is said to have been born to Devaki and Vasudeva in ancient times.

Historical Significance

The temple complex is built around the prison cell where Lord Krishna is believed to have been born. The original temple has been rebuilt several times, with the current structure dating back to the 20th century.

Visiting Information

  • Opening Hours: 5:30 AM - 12:00 PM & 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Photography restrictions apply inside temple
  • Footwear to be removed before entering

Best Time to Visit

  • October to March (Winter Season)
  • Janmashtami Festival (August/September)
